ABOUT THE PROJECT
Name
	* Summer Camp Support

Objectives
	* Introduce more youth to STEM and fabrication technology through extraordinary day camp experiences.

Description and day-to-day tasks:
	* The extern will:

- Choose four summer camps (typically serving a particular age group) to be their focus
- Assist with curriculum development, project design, materials preparation and procurement for chosen camps
- Work with makerspace staff to learn the tools required for camp projects and become confident enough to teach them to others
- Co-facilitate camps with makerspace staff

Meaningful learning about STEM careers:
	* This project will immerse the extern in the practice of exposing students to STEM careers. Projects completed in the camp setting can be modified to work in the classroom space. The extern will become familiar with best practices for introducing students to tools and advanced technology, as well as the myriad applications for these tools.	

Network growth:
	* As part of their role, externs will be encouraged to reach out to members of the makerspace and broader community with relevant expertise for the camp projects/themes.  They will also work alongside the experts and volunteers Gizmo has identified.
