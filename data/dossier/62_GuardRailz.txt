ABOUT THE PROJECT
Name
	* Enhancing Educational Outcomes through AI-Powered Prompts

Objectives
	* The extern will learn to make an educational impact by demonstrating measurable improvements in learning outcomes through AI-driven approaches. By tracking the effectiveness of AI prompts in enhancing understanding, retention, and application of knowledge, we can provide concrete evidence of our platform's value to educators and learners. This will benefit our organization by boosting educational benefits, enhancing the quality of the platform, which will ultimately boost customer satisfaction.

Meaningful learning about STEM careers:
	* Extern will apply advanced artificial intelligence technologies to address educational challenges, such as making learning more interactive and personalized. This hands-on use of technology demonstrates the transformative potential of STEM fields, inspiring students through real-world examples.

Network growth:
