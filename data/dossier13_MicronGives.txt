ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION:
Micron Gives K-12 programs are funded by the Micron Foundation to create equitable environments and inspire learning so that all students and educators can pursue a future in STEM. Programs include Summer Camps and out-of-school experiences for middle and high school students.

ABOUT THE PROJECT
Name
	* Preparing students for careers of the future

Objectives
	* Evaluate and improve existing programs that exist to inform students about career opportunities and how they can prepare for their future.		


Description and day-to-day tasks:
	* Review & update content, meeting with educators and industry professionals, deliver career events to students

Meaningful learning about STEM careers:
	* After an orientation to Micron and a period of on-boarding, externs will have direct experience with career awareness programs	


Network growth:
	* meeting with Micron professionals to collect information to improve programs
