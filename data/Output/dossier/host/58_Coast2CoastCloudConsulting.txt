ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION:
Coast 2 Coast Cloud Consulting specializes in Salesforce solutions, ensuring organizations leverage the full potential of their customer relationship management (CRM) systems. With expertise rooted in the intersection of technology and business, the consultancy embodies STEM through its application of science and technology to solve complex business challenges. It promotes STEM careers by demonstrating the transformative impact of technological expertise in the Salesforce ecosystem, aligning business strategies with digital efficiency and innovation. This practical integration of STEM disciplines showcases the vital role of technical acumen in advancing business objectives.

ABOUT THE PROJECT
Name
	* STEM Integration through Salesforce Expertise

Objectives
	* The desired outcomes of the project include enhancing Coast 2 Coast Cloud Consulting's brand recognition and authority in the Salesforce ecosystem through targeted content marketing. By enriching our digital content with educational blogs and active engagement on LinkedIn, we aim to attract and inform a wider audience. The externship will contribute fresh perspectives to our marketing strategies, leading to increased lead generation, customer engagement, and ultimately, business growth. The educator's unique insights will also help tailor our content to inspire the next generation in STEM fields.

Description and day-to-day tasks:
	* Developing a content calendar for LinkedIn posts to highlight case studies, client testimonials, and Salesforce insights.
Engaging with the STEM and Salesforce community on LinkedIn to increase network and brand visibility.
Collaborating on marketing materials to articulate the value proposition of Salesforce solutions in STEM-related fields.
Monitoring and reporting on the performance of content marketing efforts, using metrics to guide future strategies.

Meaningful learning about STEM careers:
	* Through this externship project, the extern will gain hands-on experience in applying STEM knowledge within a business context. They will see firsthand how data analytics, problem-solving, and technological solutions come to life in our Salesforce consulting work. By participating in actual projects, the extern will understand the practical applications of STEM in enhancing business operations and strategy. This real-world insight will empower them to bring tangible examples back to the classroom, enriching their teaching and igniting student interest in STEM careers.

Network growth:
	* I am the only employee of the company so they will meet with me. I am also the leader of the Boise - Salesforce Community User Group and we host monthly community meetings with guest speakers typically in the Boise area (tech company location). 

Skills desired:
	* Word Processing Software (MS Word or Google Docs);Presentation Software (MS Powerpoint or Google Slides);Secondary-Level Instructional Experience;Elementary-Level Instructional experience;Curriculum Design;Project Management;Adult Education / Training;Email & Administrative Software;

STEM domain experience desired:
	* No, experience in a specific STEM domain isn't necessary;
