ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION:
The Idaho Falls Zoo is an AZA accredited zoo, meaning we meet the highest standards of animal care, conservation, and education required for organizations that provide homes to animals and are open to the public. As part of the AZA, we are actively participating in research on local wildlife, and education practices that result in conservation action. 

ABOUT THE PROJECT
Name
	* Zoo Education Program Improvement

Objectives
	* Help to develop written procedures, program outlines, and supporting materials for Education Programs.		


Description and day-to-day tasks:
	* Participate in zoo education programs to understand the process, use Excel, Power BI, and other data tracking tools to develop resources to help track and schedule programs more efficiantly, help to outline programs and review programs to understand what practices are most sucessful in zoo programs.

Meaningful learning about STEM careers:
	* The Idaho Falls Zoo is a science focused organization. We use all parts of STEM to research, care for animals, and maintain the Zoo and keep things running smoothly. By being a part of the Zoo Externship program, externs would get a chance to see how we use STEM in the planning, implementing, and performing our educational programs.	


Network growth:
	* The extern will work closely with all the staff in the education department at the zoo including the education curator, zoo educators, and volunteer coordinator.	


Skills desired:
	* Spreadsheet Software (MS Excel or Google Sheets);Word Processing Software (MS Word or Google Docs);Curriculum Design;Public Speaking / Group Presentation;

STEM domain experience desired:
	* Life Science / Biology;Earth & Environmental Science;Computer Science (e.g., coding, programming languages, software/app development, etc.);
