Contact: Anderson, Mindi
Email: mindi@idahoveterans.org
Location: Nampa, Idaho
Remote Possible: Yes

ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION:
Our Mission is to deliver wrap-around services and a navigation network hub for veterans, active-duty military personnel, and their families, providing community resources for education, housing, entrepreneurship, workforce management, family, and wellness services.

Our Vision is to bridge the gap by building strong sustainable partnerships deeply rooted within the Idaho and Military communities through collaboration and connection. We build and maintain  relationships with businesses and organizations around Idaho to provide pathways for the military community to find the right resources every time.

ABOUT THE PROJECT
Name
	* Project Management

Objectives
	* Support the launch of our Donor Engagement Database in our current CRM Platform so we may track our funding resources and support our fund development initiatives in 2024 for a financial sustainable future. We also would like to enhance our navigation services to better serve our clients, members, and the Idaho Community at large. 

Description and day-to-day tasks:
	* Managing a database, working in Canva for marketing and graphic design, working with WIx website build, Email communications. Working with our Board to track progress in our fund development plan.

Meaningful learning about STEM careers:
	* Through our work there are many ways they could be exposed to STEM opportunities. We work with all industries. 

Network growth:
	* They would be working with myself mostly and our board of directors who serve in multiple STEM Career fields. Our team is diverse in their roles and no one person does the same thing. There area lot of ways they will meet people in different fields. 

Skills desired:
	* Spreadsheet Software (MS Excel or Google Sheets);Word Processing Software (MS Word or Google Docs);Project Management;Email & Administrative Software;

STEM domain experience desired:
	* No, experience in a specific STEM domain isn't necessary;
