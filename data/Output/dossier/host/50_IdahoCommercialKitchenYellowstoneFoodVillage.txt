ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION:
These are extensions of the Idaho Innovation Center. The Idaho Commercial Kitchen is a certified kitchen where culinary entrepreneurs can create their food in a safe and fully certified kitchen. The Yellowstone Food Village is a village of 6 foot truck spots where food truck vendors can park as their home base to establish a customer base and build their business in addition to attending events.
