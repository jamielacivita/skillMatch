ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION:
Idaho Division of Human Resources provides HR support to all state agencies across Idaho. A major function of our agency is to provide help with recruiting and retaining employees. Many of these employees and careers are in STEM fields. 

ABOUT THE PROJECT
Name
	* Public Sector Employment Exhibition

Objectives
	* The extern will provide Idaho Division of Human Resources with insight into how to better promote public sector employment pathways to students. Idaho DHR is beginning to look at recruitment strategies to address workforce gaps at the State of Idaho. Having the insight from educators on how to present these career pathways to students will be invaluable in the recruitment work the State of Idaho is doing.

Description and day-to-day tasks:
	* The day-to-day tasks of the extern will vary. Over the 6 weeks, the extern will have the opportunity to job shadow, tour, and work on projects at a variety of different agencies to expand their knowledge of state career pathways.

Meaningful learning about STEM careers:
	* The State of Idaho employs many people in STEM careers. The extern will get to see first hand many of these STEM careers and get some hands-on experience.

Network growth:
	* The extern will have the opportunity to meet individuals from many different state agencies who work in STEM careers. This may include, engineers, web developers, STEM Educators, etc.

	* No specific skills needed - we'll take anyone with the right attitude & train on what's needed!;

	* No, experience in a specific STEM domain isn't necessary;
