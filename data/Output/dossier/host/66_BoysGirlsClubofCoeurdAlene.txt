ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION:
Our organization is an after school program and summer program who values fostering hope and opportunity for youth who attend the program. STEM is one of our five core programming values. Every week we run five 45 min STEM activities. 

ABOUT THE PROJECT
Name
	* Summer of STEM 

Objectives
	* The Boys & Girls Club of Coeur d' Alene aims to fortify its educational impact through the establishment of a dedicated STEM program led by a qualified teacher. Focusing on science, technology, engineering and math. The organization seeks to actively engage its members STEM activities to both foster interest and reduce the effects of summer learning loss. By forging partnerships with local STEM resources and implementing a robust monitoring system, the club aspires to provides high-quality, hands on learning experiences and continuously enhance the effectiveness of its STEM initiatives for the benefits of its members. 

Description and day-to-day tasks:
	* Micron Extern will use in house materials to design and implement STEM based activities for youth to join. Materials include 3D printer, drones, hydro gardens, legos, keeva blocks and more.

Meaningful learning about STEM careers:
	* This project will show externs real life application of STEM and allow them to bring their skills back to the classroom, through in house developed curriculum, with STEM materials. Externs will gain skills in budgeting, planning and excuting program objectives.  

Network growth:
	* Our externs will have an opportunity to meet with club leadership and go through DIY STEM courses through Spillet Leadership University. 

Skills desired:
	* Curriculum Design;Project Management;Public Speaking / Group Presentation;Elementary-Level Instructional experience;No specific skills needed - we'll take anyone with the right attitude & train on what's needed!;

STEM domain experience desired:
	* No, experience in a specific STEM domain isn't necessary;
