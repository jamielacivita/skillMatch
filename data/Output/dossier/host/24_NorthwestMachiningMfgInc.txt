ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION:
Northwest Machining & Mfg is a manufacturer of Aerospace and defense machine shop with full programming, CNC lathe and mill machine 

ABOUT THE PROJECT
Name
	* Trade for Trade

Objectives
	* Our end goal with this project is to educated and create interest in machining and manufacturing industry so educators, school guidance counselors can help promote these types of careers to students for future consideration. Our organization is looking for the long term return by establishing key relationships within our schools, community and bridging businesses with students and educators. Additionally we will be looking to open a trades program training center to help provide grant and scholarship trades programs to students and graduates.  

Description and day-to-day tasks:
	* Extern will be working on learning all aspects of the business ie: shipping, receiving, purchasing, CNC machining, manual machining, inspection, programming and project engagement to understand what and how machine shops impact programs like space and aerospace industries.

Meaningful learning about STEM careers:
	* This project is a very hands on learning emersion. We will have the extern work directly with employees in different areas, learning how to run machines, program, estimate, and learn how engineering goes from concept to tangible product. 

Network growth:
	* We have many different departments and specialized employees with expertise in welding, CAD programming, CMM inspection, manual and CNC machinists, accounting and quality assurance. We will be moving the extern throughout the shop and expose him to as many areas as we can. 

Skills desired:
	* No specific skills needed - we'll take anyone with the right attitude & train on what's needed!;Any manufacturing experience is a huge plus;

STEM domain experience desired:
	* Trades & Industry (e.g. manufacturing, automotive, construction, etc.);Engineering & Technology;
