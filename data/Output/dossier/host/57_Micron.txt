ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION:
The US Expansion team is planning, building and operating Micron's latest advanced manufacturing facility in Boise, Idaho.  The team consists of engineering, construction, and manufacturing functions.  

ABOUT THE PROJECT
Name
	* Micron's Idaho expansion internal project communication strategy

Objectives
	* Develop an internal communication plan for Micron's Idaho expansion.  
Educate the team on the scope of the project and progress.  
Build excitement and engagement for the overall team.
Create a template that can continue to be used following the externship.

Description and day-to-day tasks:
	* Interview experts across the entire project to capture facts and progress of the project across all Engineering and Construction teams.  Create digital graphics and visuals that quickly communicates these key facts.  Incorporate these digital images, graphics in presentations and on the internal website.

Meaningful learning about STEM careers:
	* The extern will get to see STEM skills used across a major construction and manufacturing project.  They will get a "behind the scenes" look at many of the key facts and figures about the project.

Network growth:
	* The extern will get exposure to all aspects of the Micron expansion project that touches every engineering discipline.  Construction, civil engineering, mechanical, electrical, industrial, chemical, etc.  

Skills desired:
	* Presentation Software (MS Powerpoint or Google Slides);Email & Administrative Software;Digital design software to make images/infographics;

STEM domain experience desired:
	* Prefer someone with technical aptitude and strong creative/digital design expertise;
