ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION:
We are a 501(c)5 organization that provides outreach, collaboration and education to the general public regarding Organized Labor and Unions in the state of Idaho. We provide resources and tools to Union Locals for internal and external organizing and community outreach. We facilitate registered apprenticeship program outreach between Joint Apprenticeship Training Centers and job seekers of all ages. Our Unions are present in all STEM job sectors, including engineering, manufacturing and design. 

ABOUT THE PROJECT
Name
	* Develop the process and plan for "Organizing Institute" and Community Action		

Objectives
	* The extern will assist the COPE, Communications and Data Program Managers in developing and leading the "Organizing Institute" Class & Developing a Community Action Campaign for member to member outreach		


Description and day-to-day tasks:
	* Using the Labor Action Network to analyze membership demographics

Meaningful learning about STEM careers:
	* We will show our extern a wide range of careers and tools available in organized Labor, how to develop a plan for communications and community action that they can pass along to students interested in registered apprenticeship programs as well as traditional education pathways	


Network growth:
	* The extern will collaborate with the Communications, Organizing & leadership teams from over 90 Union Locals in Idaho. Throughout the externship, the teacher extern will contribute to projects that will give them tools and resources that will provide ongoing relationships with various indutrial, trades and public Unions. These relationships can be utilized to further their students' career goals long after graduation.	


Skills desired:
	* No specific skills needed - we'll take anyone with the right attitude & train on what's needed!;

STEM domain experience desired:
	* No, experience in a specific STEM domain isn't necessary;
