Organization: Idaho Division of Vocational Rehabilitation
Contact: Monaghan, MiKayla
Email: mikayla.monaghan@vr.idaho.gov
Location: Boise, Idaho
Remote Possible: No

ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION:
IDVR supports students, adults to be successful in employment while meeting the needs of businesses. IDVR supports individuals to enter into STEM careers, supports CTE summer camps for students with disabilities, and works with businesses in STEM fields to support them in reaching their business goals. 

ABOUT THE PROJECT
Name
	* Learn about IDVR

Objectives
	* To understand the IDVR program to be able to educate others including students on how IDVR may be a great program to explore. This will benefit our agency by having a teacher in the schools with better understanding and awareness of the IDVR program. 

Description and day-to-day tasks:
	* shadowing IDVR staff, shadowing IDVR summer programs, in general, learning about the IDVR program as a whole.

Meaningful learning about STEM careers:
	* Extern will exit the summer understanding the IDVR program as a whole (who we serve and the basics of how) 

Network growth:
	* Extern will network with the IDVR staff that work with their school district

Skills desired:
	* No specific skills needed - we'll take anyone with the right attitude & train on what's needed!;

STEM domain experience desired:
	* No, experience in a specific STEM domain isn't necessary;
