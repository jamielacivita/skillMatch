Organization: International Rescue Committee
Contact: Balogh, Pippa
Email: pippa.balogh@rescue.org
Location: Boise, Idaho
Remote Possible: No

ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION:
The International Rescue Committee is a refugee resettlement agency.  The IRC is invested in helping newly arrived refugees have a strong foundation to start their lives in the USA.  The Mission of the IRC is to help people whose lives have been shattered by conflict and disaster, including the climate crisis, to survive, recover and gain control over their future.

ABOUT THE PROJECT
Name
	* Program and Client Services (Cash Assistance)

Objectives
	* Support IRC Boise cash assistance teams with cash assistance enrollments within program deadlines and help recently arrived refugees access federal cash assistance benefits related to personal use and housing use.		

Description and day-to-day tasks:
	* Assist the cash assistance teams at IRC Boise with cash assistance pre-enrollment analysis's. Screen recently arrived refugees for the two cash assistance programs at IRC Boise and communicate outcomes.

Meaningful learning about STEM careers:
	* "•	Extern will gain insight into refugee and asylee workforce integration programs.
•	Extern will learn to create and track documentation and gain experience with time management.
•	Extern will learn to communicate effectively with clients across cultures and with interpreters. 
•	Extern will learn effective outreach strategies for building strategic relationships.
"	

Network growth:
	* The extern will learn to communicate effectively with clients across cultures and with interpreters, by conducting pre-enrollment analyses with eligible clients and assist with cash assistance enrollment processes. Extern will be directly assisting/supporting refugee families/clients in their resettlement journey in Idaho.	

Skills desired:
	* No specific skills needed - we'll take anyone with the right attitude & train on what's needed!;Email & Administrative Software;Universal communication skills & experience with excel.;

STEM domain experience desired:
	* No, experience in a specific STEM domain isn't necessary;
